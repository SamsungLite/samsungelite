INVESTOR FAQ

How are daily profits generated? ▼
Our profits come directly from Samsung electronics sales across our 8 authorized stores in the USA. TVs, smartphones, appliances — we sell premium Samsung products daily with high margins. A portion of these sales revenue is distributed to investors as daily profits, making this a real, tangible investment in retail operations.
When do I start receiving my daily profits? ▼
Your profits begin from the business day following the confirmation of your investment. Our team will activate your account within 30 minutes of funding, and your first profit will be credited to your dashboard by 8:00 PM on day 1.
How do I withdraw my profits? ▼
Withdrawals are instant and available 24/7 through your investor dashboard. You can withdraw via US bank transfer (same-day), Bitcoin, USDT, Ethereum, or mobile money. There are no withdrawal fees and no minimum withdrawal amount.
Is my investment capital safe? ▼
Yes. All investor capital is held in a licensed escrow account managed by our financial partner, completely separate from business operations. Your principal is insured and returned 100% at the end of your 30-day investment cycle, regardless of business performance.
Can I invest in multiple plans simultaneously? ▼
Yes! Elite plan investors have access to multiple plan stacking — you can run several active investments simultaneously to multiply your daily earnings. Growth and Starter plan investors can upgrade or open a second plan after their first 30-day cycle completes.
What happens after the 30-day cycle ends? ▼
At the end of your 30-day investment cycle, you receive your full principal back plus all accrued daily profits. You then have the choice to reinvest your principal (plus earnings) into a new cycle — many investors choose this to compound their returns significantly.
